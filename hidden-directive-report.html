INITIAL ACCESS

The AI Tool Became the Attack Surface_

⚡ NOVEL VECTOR

SITUATION: Greenfield ran an internal AI page-summarizer — a scheduled python.exe process that fetches and summarizes external pages. The actor got it to fetch an attacker-controlled page whose contents were command-injected into the tool, disabling AMSI and spawning a PowerShell loader that pulled and injected an AdaptixC2 implant. The AI tool wasn't compromised by malware — it was talked into attacking its own host.

REMEDIATION

What Gets Fixed First_

⚠ SINGLE MOST IMPORTANT ACTION

Rotate the krbtgt password twice to invalidate the Golden Ticket. Until that happens, the attacker can authenticate as any user or service in greenfield.local — every other remediation step is secondary to this one.

Contain

Isolate all three hosts, preserve for forensics, rebuild GF-DC01 and GF-SRV01 from known-good media.

Reset Everything

Disable sancadmin, svc_backup, t.harris, d.williams; force a domain-wide password reset.

Quarantine the AI Tool

Take the page-summarizer offline until it's re-architected to treat fetched content as data, never instructions.

Harden Identity

LSASS protection, SMB signing, restrict Type-3 logons for service accounts, monitor DCSync rights.
