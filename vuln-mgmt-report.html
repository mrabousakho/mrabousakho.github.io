METHODOLOGY

Policy, Buy-In, and Sign-Off_

PHASE 1

Policy Draft

Drafted a Vulnerability Management Policy outlining scope, responsibilities, and remediation timelines.

PHASE 2

Stakeholder Buy-In

Mock meeting with the server team extended the critical remediation window from 48 hours to one week.

PHASE 3

Leadership Sign-Off

Revised policy received final approval from senior leadership, giving it authority to guide the program.

PHASE 4

Scan Permission

Negotiated a single-server pilot scan with just-in-time AD credentials for controlled, monitored access.

⚡ PROGRAM SCOPE

INCEPTION STATE: the organization has no existing policy or vulnerability management practices in place. COMPLETION STATE: a formal policy is enacted, stakeholder buy-in is secured, and a full cycle of organization-wide vulnerability remediation is successfully completed. Technology used: Tenable, Azure Virtual Machines (Nessus), and PowerShell/Bash remediation scripts.

REMEDIATION_CYCLE

Four Rounds, Verified Each Time_

After assessment and prioritization, vulnerabilities were remediated in priority order and confirmed with a follow-up scan each round.
