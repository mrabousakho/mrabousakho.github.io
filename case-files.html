Hunt Clinic_

A deeper walkthrough of method, not just findings — how I actually worked the case, including the dead ends.

clinic__northpeak_descent.md
> clinic --case northpeak_descent --show methodology

Triage first. Before touching a single flag, I scoped the alert queue and grouped events by host and account, so I wasn't chasing eighteen separate stories — I was chasing one attacker across eighteen checkpoints.

Attempt before answer. Every query in this hunt was written and run by me first. Where I got stuck, I documented exactly what telemetry was missing rather than guessing — that gap is part of the report, not hidden from it.

Result: 18/18 flags, one of four perfect scores on the range, and a repeatable KQL pattern I now reuse across dual-SIEM cases like Hidden Directive.
